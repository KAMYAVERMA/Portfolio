FoodDole

The focus of this project is to reduce the amount of food wasted and being used to the needy people.
Developed a food donation platform wherein a person as a donar can donate her/his leftover food to poors and fill their stomach.
The platform for food donation acts as an interface between the users who are looking for a channel to give the excess food without wasting it. It enables us to donate the excess food by notifying nearby users with the details of the food that is available.
